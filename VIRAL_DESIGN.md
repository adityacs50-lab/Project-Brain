## Brand & Style
This is the **Viral Editorial System** for Company Brain. It combines the prestige of a high-end magazine like *The Economist* or *Wired* with the high-velocity engagement of Medium. 

The aesthetic is **Content-First Minimalism**. We use generous white space, stunning oversized imagery, and masterful typography to signal authority. It's designed to make the reader feel they are consuming "insider knowledge" rather than just another blog post.

## Key Principles
1. **The Hook**: Headlines use serif fonts (Playfair Display) to feel "earned" and authoritative.
2. **Visual Magnetism**: Every post must have a custom, high-fidelity AI-generated image.
3. **Retention Architecture**: Short paragraphs, large line heights, and clear sub-headers ensure readers reach the end.
4. **Social Proof**: Subtle but visible "Clap" and "Share" counts that feel like badges of honor.
