# 📢 The Thiel Distribution Strategy: Company Brain

**Goal:** To achieve the "Last Mover Advantage" by dominating a niche and building a viral distribution loop.

---

## 1. The Trojan Horse: "The Policy Leak Auditor"
**Concept:** A free, high-value tool that proves the problem exists before we sell the solution.
*   **The Offer:** "Upload your Slack export (1 week) and get a 'Logic Health Report' in 60 seconds."
*   **The Hook:** We show them 3 places where their team is giving conflicting advice to customers.
*   **The Conversion:** "To fix these leaks and enforce these rules automatically, upgrade to the Company Brain Control Plane."

## 2. The $10k Pilot Sales Script (The "Thiel" Pitch)
*   **The Opening:** "Most companies think AI agents are the future. They are wrong. Controlled AI agents are the future. Currently, your agents are guessing. We give them a Brain."
*   **The Secret:** "The secret is that your institutional knowledge isn't in your documentation; it's in your Slack history. We are the only ones who can turn that 'noise' into 'law'."
*   **The Pricing:** Never charge per user. Charge **Per Rule Enforced.** This aligns your profit with their success. Start at $1,000/month for the first 10 rules.

## 3. The Viral Loop: "Standardized Logic"
*   **Mechanism:** When a company creates a world-class "Security Policy" rule, they can "Publish" it to the Global Brain (Anonymized).
*   **The Moat:** Other companies join just to see what the "Best-in-Class" companies are doing. Every new user makes the "Global Brain" smarter, making it impossible for a competitor to catch up.
